Recommendations

#1

Alleppey Backwaters

Venice of the East · Houseboat Paradise

🌡️ 25°-32°C 📅 Best: Nov-Feb

🍽️ Must Try:

Karimeen Pollichathu (pearl spot fish wrapped in banana leaf), Kerala Sadya (24-dish banana leaf feast), Alleppey Fish Curry, Kappa & Meen (tapioca with fish), Toddy.

🎯 Activities:

Glide through palm-fringed canals on a traditional Kettuvallam houseboat, explore village paths between paddy fields, and witness golden sunset over Vembanad Lake framed by coconut groves.

#2

Munnar Hills

Tea Garden Paradise · Misty Mountains

🌡️ 15°-22°C 📅 Best: Oct-Mar

🍽️ Must Try:

Appam with Stew (creamy coconut milk curry), Puttu with Kadala Curry, Kairali Thali, Fresh Tea from local plantations, Homemade chocolates.

🎯 Activities:

Wander through endless emerald tea carpets blanketed in mountain mist, visit Eravikulam National Park home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr, and watch clouds roll in at Echo Point (1700m elevation).

#3

Varkala Beach

Cliffside Paradise · Spiritual Haven

🌡️ 28°-33°C 📅 Best: Oct-Mar

🍽️ Must Try:

Grilled Seafood at cliff-top cafes, Fish Moilee (coconut fish curry), Kerala Prawn Curry, Avial (mixed vegetable coconut dish), Palada Payasam (rice pudding).

🎯 Activities:

Join seaside yoga on dramatic red laterite cliffs with the Arabian Sea stretching before you, parasail over turquoise waters, and visit the ancient Janardhana Swamy Temple.

#4

Wayanad

Forest Paradise · Spice Country

🌡️ 18°-25°C 📅 Best: Oct-Feb

🍽️ Must Try:

Bamboo Rice Payasam, Puttu with Kadala Curry, Karimeen Fry, Wayanadan Kozhi Curry (chicken), Ela Ada (leaf-wrapped sweet).

🎯 Activities:

Trek through misty forests to ancient Edakkal Caves with 6000-year-old petroglyphs, embark on a wildlife safari, and visit the stunning Banasura Sagar Dam surrounded by mountains.

#5

Kochi

Queen of Arabian Sea · Cultural Hub

🌡️ 28°-35°C 📅 Best: Oct-Mar

🍽️ Must Try:

Malabar Biryani, Meen Curry (spicy fish curry), Koonthal Roast (squid), Pazham Pori (banana fritters), Sulaimani Tea.

🎯 Activities:

Explore Fort Kochi's colonial streets, watch Chinese fishing nets at sunset, experience a live Kathakali dance performance, and wander through fragrant spice markets in Mattancherry.

Visual Journey

Kerala Frames

Alleppey — the Venice of the East. Glide through tranquil backwaters on a traditional houseboat, surrounded by lush paddy fields, coconut groves, and village life untouched by time.

Munnar — a breathtaking hill station blanketed in endless tea gardens, misty valleys, and winding trails through the Western Ghats.

Varkala — a stunning cliffside beach where golden sands meet dramatic red laterite cliffs, offering breathtaking sunsets and a serene spiritual vibe.

Wayanad — a lush green paradise of misty forests, cascading waterfalls, spice plantations, and ancient caves nestled in the Western Ghats.

Kochi — the Queen of the Arabian Sea, where colonial heritage meets modern vibrancy. Famous for its Chinese fishing nets, spice markets, and rich cultural tapestry.
